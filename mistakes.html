5 Common Mistakes Parking Lot Owners Make

  • Relying on attendants who increase costs and create inconsistencies
  • Using outdated gate arms that break down and frustrate drivers
  • Failing to enforce properly, leading to unpaid usage
  • Ignoring occupancy data and pricing opportunities
  • Delaying adoption of automation until revenue has already declined
