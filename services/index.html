Which Solution Fits Your Property?

Each property is different. Our consultants help you choose between Scan to Pay and LPR based on your specific needs, or implement both for maximum flexibility.

Scan to Pay

Common Use Cases

  • Customer-facing businesses (retail, restaurants, medical offices)
  • Properties with lower historical violation rates
  • Locations requiring quick deployment
  • Budget-conscious initial implementations

Key Features

  • Fast launch and minimal investment
  • Works on any smartphone (no app required)
  • Built-in grace periods and clear customer communication
  • Payment history creates instant audit trails
OR COMBINE BOTH

Some properties use both solutions: Scan to Pay and LPR

Our consultants help determine the right mix for your property.

LPR (License Plate Recognition)

Common Use Cases

  • Residential or employee parking areas
  • Properties with higher historical violation rates
  • Tow-enforced locations
  • Situations requiring hands-off enforcement

Key Features

  • Automated plate capture with photo proof
  • Zero customer interaction needed
  • Consistent enforcement 24/7
  • Complete documentation for disputes

How the Selection Process Works

Our consultants analyze six key factors to recommend the optimal solution for your property's unique situation.

Layout & Traffic Flow

Physical property design, entry/exit points, and typical traffic patterns.

Duration & Turnover

Average parking duration and how frequently spaces turn over.

Violation Rate

Current abuse levels, freeloading patterns, and enforcement challenges.

Budget & Timeline

Available investment and desired launch timeframe.

Staffing Resources

Available personnel for monitoring and enforcement support.

Local Requirements

Municipal regulations, signage laws, and compliance needs.
