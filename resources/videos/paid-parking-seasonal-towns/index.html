Is Paid Parking Worth It in Seasonal or Tourist Towns?

Dave breaks down why seasonal and tourist towns are ideal for parking monetization. When visitors walk around town and leave their cars parked for hours, you can generate tens of thousands of dollars in just a few months of peak season. Learn why this simple behavior pattern makes tourist destinations some of the best opportunities for parking revenue.

About This Video

Property owners in seasonal and tourist towns often wonder if paid parking makes sense when their busy season only lasts a few months. Dave answers with a resounding yes - and the math backs him up.

He explains the economics of tourist-town parking: visitors arrive, park their cars, and spend hours shopping, dining, and exploring. During those hours, your lot is generating revenue automatically. Even with just 3-4 months of peak season, the numbers can be substantial. Dave walks through real scenarios showing how properties in seasonal destinations can generate tens of thousands of dollars during their busy months, making the investment worthwhile even with an off-season lull.
